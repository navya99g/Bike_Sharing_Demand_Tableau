# Key Insights – Bike Rental Analysis


## Demand Patterns
- Bike rentals peak during **working hours (8–10 AM, 5–7 PM)**.
- **Summer season** shows the highest rental volume across all hours.

## Seasonal Trends
- Rentals steadily increase from spring and peak in summer.
- Winter shows significantly lower usage due to weather conditions.

## Weather Impact
- **Clear weather** contributes to the highest rentals.
- **Snowy and rainy conditions** lead to reduced demand.

## Operational Insights
- Weekdays have higher rentals compared to holidays.
- High-demand hours align with office commute timings.
